Our Story

The Development of EM

Our Journey from Creative Hub to Marketing Agency
01

Our Evolution

Expressive Marketing began as a creative hub at the University of Florida, uniting students passionate about art, film, music, and technology. Over time, EM evolved into a multifaceted marketing agency, leveraging its diverse talents to craft compelling campaigns that resonate with modern audiences. Its transformation reflects a commitment to innovation and collaboration, blending artistic expression with strategic marketing expertise.

02

Early Growth & Success

In its early days, EM launched a highly effective advertising campaign to grow its membership, targeting UF students through social media, campus events, and partnerships with other organizations. Engaging content, from striking visuals to relatable videos, highlighted EM's unique value as a space for interdisciplinary creativity and professional growth. These efforts brought in over 500 members in just two months, setting a strong foundation for its continued expansion.

03

Today's Impact

Today, EM's brand remains vibrant and influential, consistently attracting new members while serving as a creative force for projects like promoting Heather, a campus app, and other initiatives. The organization continues to thrive as a platform where creativity meets purpose, building a community that inspires and empowers its members.
