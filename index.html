Campaign Highlights

Gathr

Gathr is a mobile app built to boost student involvement on college campuses by making it easy to discover clubs, events, and more. EM played a key role in shaping the app's launch strategy, branding, and outreach efforts. With support from EM, Gathr launched with over 3,000 users, quickly becoming a go-to platform for campus life.

Bricks & Minifigs

Through our partnership with Bricks & Minifigs, EM is leading the marketing efforts to grow their brand presence and drive foot traffic by spotlighting LEGO®-themed events, creative content, and community engagement. We're helping Bricks & Minifigs become the place where LEGO® lovers come together to build, connect, and create.

Bite of Power

Bite of Power serves up delicious, well crafted meals designed to fuel students at any time of the day. Whether you're looking for a quick bite or a satisfying meal, this hidden gem is redefining campus dining with fresh, flavorful options for everyone. EM is working on rebranding the business to increase foot traffic and help bring up their revenu for the new quarter.
